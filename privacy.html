Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to DailyRunner.Fun ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding information security and privacy when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

We collect minimal information automatically during your visit to our website:

  • IP Addresses: Your IP address is temporarily stored in memory (not persistent storage) for security purposes, specifically to implement rate limiting that prevents denial-of-service attacks.
  • User-Agent Headers: We check your browser's User-Agent string against known malicious patterns for security purposes. This information is not stored.
  • Server Logs: Our server may automatically log basic information about requests, including IP addresses, timestamps, requested URLs, and HTTP status codes. These logs are used solely for technical troubleshooting and security monitoring.

2.2 Information You Provide

We do not collect any personal information directly from you. Our website does not have:

  • User accounts or profiles
  • Forms for collecting personal data
  • Contact forms
  • Newsletter sign-ups
  • Comment sections

3. Cookies and Tracking

3.1 No Cookies

We do not use cookies on our website. This includes:

  • No session cookies
  • No persistent cookies
  • No third-party cookies

3.2 No Tracking Technologies

We do not use:

  • Web beacons
  • Tracking pixels
  • Website analytics tools (like Google Analytics)
  • Social media widgets that may collect data
  • Advertising identifiers

4. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively for:

4.1 Security Purposes

  • DDoS protection
  • Rate limiting
  • Request size limiting
  • Blocking suspicious traffic

4.2 Technical Administration

  • Server maintenance
  • Troubleshooting
  • Improving website performance

5. Data Retention

5.1 IP Addresses

  • Temporary storage in server memory only (not persistent)
  • Automatically deleted after 60 seconds (our rate limiting window)
  • If rate limits are exceeded, the IP may be temporarily blocked for up to 5 minutes
  • All IP records are deleted once the rate limiting window or blocking period expires

5.2 Server Logs

  • Retained for a maximum of 7 days
  • Used only for security and technical troubleshooting purposes
  • Automatically deleted after the retention period

6. Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any information with third parties.

6.1 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

7. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information. These measures include:

  • Security headers (X-Content-Type-Options, X-Frame-Options, X-XSS-Protection, Referrer-Policy)
  • Request size limiting
  • Rate limiting
  • IP filtering
  • User-Agent filtering

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. Since we collect minimal data that is temporarily stored for security purposes only, most traditional data subject rights do not apply. However, you may have the right to:

  • Access information about what data is collected
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we might have collected information from a child, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date.

11. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you are accessing our website from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. By using our website, you consent to this transfer.
